THE STORY

◆ The Rise and Fall of Bon's Burgers ◆

A Dream Born in 1958

The idea of Bon's Burgers began in 1958, when two college students, Jack Walten and Felix Kranken, shared the idea of a restaurant that had animated performers [[1]].

After years of development, Bon's Burgers officially opened on June 28, 1974 in Brighton, Wisconsin [[25]]. The restaurant quickly became a local favorite, known for its delicious food and entertaining animatronic shows.

The Showstoppers — Bon, Banny, Sha, and Boozoo — became beloved characters among children and families alike [[6]]. Created by Bunny Smiles Incorporated, the restaurant lasted from June 28, 1974 to the 20th of 1982 [[27]].
